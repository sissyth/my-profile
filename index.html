Our Mission

Gorillas are among the most endangered animals on our planet. The destruction of their natural habitat, hunting, and disease have led to a dramatic decline in their population. In fact, it is estimated that there are only a few thousand gorillas left in the world. It is our responsibility to act now to prevent their extinction.

That's why we created this website, to raise awareness among the general public about the importance of protecting gorillas and to raise funds to finance projects aimed at safeguarding these magnificent animals. With your help, we can fund research programs, habitat conservation actions, and efforts to combat poaching.

By making a donation, you can help protect these fascinating creatures and preserve their natural habitat for future generations. Together, we can make a difference and ensure that gorillas continue to thrive in the wild!
